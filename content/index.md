## About Me

I'm Thanh Vo, currently working at **[Trusting Social](https://trustingsocial.com/)**, where I focus on building production-ready AI systems that solve real-world problems with Large Language Models (LLMs).

My journey in science and technology began with a passion for **physics** at Luong Van Chanh High School for The Gifted in Phu Yen (now Dak Lak). One of my proudest achievements was earning **Second Prize at the Vietnam National Physics Olympiad**, along with other national and provincial awards (aside from my "cựu thiếu nhi" achievement). 

> 📰 [Read more about my achievements](https://phuyen.baodaklak.vn/79/215684/bang-vang-thanh-tich-hoc-tap-cua-thanh.html)

From physics to machine learning, my path has been driven by **curiosity** about how systems work, whether understanding the fundamental laws of nature or building intelligent AI systems. Today, I apply that same analytical mindset to develop cutting-edge machine learning solutions.
